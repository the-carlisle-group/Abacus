AlertBox Show Method

Displays an alert box.

   R←I AlertBox.Show ''

or

   R←AlertBox.Show I

I is the AlertBox instance. R is '0'.

This method does not wait, and generally should be the last line of a callback, with no following code.

See the HTMLDocument Alert method for easiest use of the AlertBox, encapsulating the New and Show methods.
